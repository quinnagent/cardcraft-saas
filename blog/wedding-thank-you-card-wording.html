50 Wedding Thank You Card Wording Examples for Every Situation

Writing wedding thank you cards can feel overwhelming, especially when you're trying to find the right words for dozens (or hundreds!) of guests. Don't worry - we've compiled over 50 examples you can copy, customize, and make your own.

For Cash Gifts

Example 1 - Formal

"Dear [Name], Thank you so much for your generous gift. Your thoughtfulness means the world to us as we begin our married life together. We plan to use it toward [specific goal like our first home or honeymoon]. We're so grateful you could share our special day with us. With love, [Your Names]"

Example 2 - Warm & Personal

"[Name], We can't thank you enough for your incredibly generous gift! It means so much to us as we start this new chapter. We've already started planning how to use it to build our life together. Thank you for being there on our special day. Love, [Your Names]"

For Physical Gifts

Example 3 - Kitchen Gift

"Dear [Name], Thank you so much for the beautiful [gift]. We can't wait to use it in our new kitchen! Every time we [use it/cook with it], we'll think of you and your thoughtfulness. Thank you for celebrating with us! Love, [Your Names]"

Example 4 - Home Decor

"[Name], The [gift] is absolutely perfect for our home! We love how it [matches our style/will look in our living room]. Thank you for your generosity and for being part of our wedding day. With appreciation, [Your Names]"

For Guests Who Traveled

Example 5 - Acknowledging Distance

"Dear [Name], We know you traveled [distance] to be with us on our wedding day, and that means more than words can express. Thank you for making the journey and for your thoughtful gift. Having you there made our day truly special. Love, [Your Names]"

Tips for Writing Thank You Cards

  • Be specific about the gift
  • Mention how you'll use it
  • Reference the wedding day
  • Keep it personal but concise
  • Send within 3 months of the wedding
